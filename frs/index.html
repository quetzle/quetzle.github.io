THE PATENT-PENDING PROCEDURE IS A PAINLESS, DAILY PROCESS TO CULTIVATE FORESKIN GROWTH. THREE TYPES OF MEDICATION ARE USED: INTRAVENOUS INJECTION, PILL, AND TOPICAL OINTMENT. THESE MEDICATIONS ARE USED IN A 4-STEP PROCESS: IMMUNOSUPPRESSION, FOUNDATION, CONSTRUCTION, AND REHABILITATION.
  1. IMMUNOSUPPRESSION: AN IMMUNOSUPPRESSIVE MEDICATION KNOWN AS CYCLOSPORIN IS ADMINISTERED INTRAVENOUSLY INTO A PERIPHERAL VEIN, USUALLY ON THE PATIENT'S ARM. THIS STEP IS HIGHLY IMPORTANT AS IT PREVENTS THE BODY FROM REJECTING THE NEW FORESKIN. CYCLOSPORIN IS TAKEN DAILY THROUGHOUT THE TREATMENT PROCESS.
  2. FOUNDATION: A FOUNDATION MUST BE BUILT SO THAT A FORESKIN CAN BE REGROWN SAFELY. A STEM CELL OINTMENT KNOWN AS MONOMNICIN (COLLOQUIALLY KNOWN IN THE BUSINESS AS "FETUS OIL") IS APPLIED TO THE CIRCUMCIZED SKIN. MONOMNICIN WORKS TO REBUILD SEVERED NERVE ENDINGS IN THE CIRCUMCIZED AREA. FOLLOWING APPLICATION OF FETUS OIL, PATIENTS SHOULD AVOID AROUSING SITUATIONS AND MATERIAL SUCH AS PORNOGRAPHY OR ATTRACTIVE WOMEN. ERECTIONS CAN BE PAINFUL AS THEY STRETCH THE SENSITIVE SKIN AROUND THE REGROWN NERVES. PATIENTS SHOULD BE PUT ON A SOY-BASED DIET TO LOWER RISK OF ERECTION. ANTIDEPRESSANTS SUCH AS ZOLOFT CAN REDUCE LIBIDO AND SHOULD BE PRESCRIBED IF PATIENT IS AT HIGH RISK OF ERECTION. THE FOUNDATION STEP IS USUALLY COMPLETED IN NO LONGER THAN ONE WEEK.
  3. CONSTRUCTION: WHEN NERVE ENDINGS ARE SUCCESSFULLY REGROWN ON THE EPITHELIUM OF THE PENIS, CONSTRUCTION OF THE FORESKIN CAN BEGIN. A SYNTHETIC SCAFFOLD IS ATTACHED TO THE CIRCUMCIZED AREA TO PROMOTE PROPER REGROWTH OF BOTH SKIN AND BLOOD VESSELS. DEPENDING ON THE CIRCUMFERENCE AND SIZE OF THE GLANS PENIS, THREE TO FIVE SCAFFOLDS MUST BE PLACED SEQUENTIALLY ON THE FORESKIN AS IT IS REGROWN. VITAMIN C SUPPLEMENTS ARE STRONGLY RECOMMENDED. CONSTRUCTION SHOULD BE COMPLETED IN TWO TO THREE WEEKS.
  4. REHABILITATION: GOOD NUTRITION IS OF UTMOST IMPORTANCE IN MAINTAINING A YOUTHFUL FORESKIN. RECOMMENDED SUPPLEMENTS AND FOODS: VITAMIN C, HUMAN BREAST MILK, AND PROTEIN. A SPECIALLY-CRAFTED PAINKILLER KNOWN AS VIRIDONE SPECIFICALLY TARGETS THE NEW FORESKIN. IN SPECIAL CASES, OTHER PRESCRIPTION PAINKILLERS MAY BE TAKEN. A SPECIALLY-LUBRICATED CONDOM (LUBE CONTAINS ALOE VERA AND PAINT THINNER) SHOULD BE WORN AND CHANGED DAILY. THOUGH THE FORESKIN HAS BEEN FULLY CONSTRUCTED, REHABILITATION CAN TAKE FOUR TO SIX WEEKS. AFTER THE REHABILITATION PERIOD, MEDICAL ATTENTION IS GENERALLY NO LONGER REQUIRED.
